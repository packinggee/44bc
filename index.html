What is 44BC?

44BC is a collection born from the most pivotal moment in human history — the assassination of Julius Caesar in 44 BC, the year an empire trembled and the ancient world changed forever.

This collection is 444 unique, hand-crafted tokens deployed on Ethereum through OpenSea. Every piece is singular — no rares, no commons. In this senate, every senator holds equal standing. Each token is a relic. Each holder, a citizen of the eternal city.

We launched 44BC to immortalize that moment of fracture, rebirth, and legacy — translating the raw power of ancient Rome into the permanence of the blockchain.

Why 44BC?
